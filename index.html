About

Product Manager @ BarnTools

Motto - "Build something people want", "Move fast and break things", and "Talk to users"

AK, product manager at BarnTools, an Agritech startup based out of Iowa, has 5 years’ experience working in the area of Analytics, including as Senior Analyst - Data Science at UnitedHealth Group, Advanced Analytics Analyst at Cambia Health, Business Intelligence Analyst at Chella Software, and Engineering Consultant at Tata Consultancy Services.

As PM, AK leads the development of BarnTalk, an AI-powered smart alarm platform for the livestock industry, that enables farm owners and enterprises monitor and protect their farm, all from the palm of their hands.

AK received his Master of Science Degree from Carnegie Mellon University majoring in Information Systems. He has a Bachelor of Engineering degree from Thiagarajar College of Engineering, India majoring in Mechanical Engineering.

AK resides in Des Moines, IA and enjoys playing badminton and speed solving Rubik’s cube
