About

Co-founder - ChapterMe & PrepAir

Motto - "Build something people want", "Move fast and break things", and "Write code - Talk to users"

Annamalai Kathir is currently a co-founder at PrepAir, a web-app that helps customers turn their video pitches into measurable results (slides, customers, cash flow, and more). He has over 4 years’ of experience working in the area of Product and Analytics, including as Product Manager at BarnTools (Agritech startup), Data Scientist at UnitedHealth Group and Cambia Health, Business Intelligence Analyst at Chella Software, and Engineering Consultant at Tata Engineering Services.

As a previous founder, Annamalai Kathir built ChapterMe, that helped YouTubers and Podcasters to quickly create timestamped chapters for their content in one click. He grew the company to $5K MRR and 15 paying customers, including YC, in less than a month.

Annamalai Kathir received his Master of Science Degree from Carnegie Mellon University, majoring in Information Systems. He has a Bachelor of Engineering degree from Thiagarajar College of Engineering, India majoring in Mechanical Engineering.

Annamalai Kathir resides in Chicago, IL and enjoys playing badminton and speed solving Rubik’s cube.
